# Availability Responses
AVAILABLE:
“We have **5+** in stock for the {product} in {color}/{size}.”

LOW-STOCK (alt color):
“That exact pick is available, and **{other_color}** is **low in stock** right now.”

OOS + Restock:
“{product} in {color}/{size} is out of stock. Restock in about **2 weeks**. Prefer **email** or **SMS** for a notification?”
